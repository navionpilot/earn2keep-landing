Support

Help & FAQ

Quick answers to the questions we hear most. Can't find what you're looking for? Email us at support@earn2keep.com — a real person reads every message.

For Organizers

What does it cost to run a fundraiser?
$149 per Camp (single team) or $299 per Tournament (multiple teams). Plus a 3.5% transaction & hosting fee on the funds you raise — this covers payment processing and platform infrastructure. We don't take a cut of your fundraising on top of that. No hidden setup fees, no per-player charges.
How long can an event run?
Up to 30 days. Most groups find that 2–3 weeks is the sweet spot — long enough for momentum, short enough to keep kids focused.
Can I run a fundraiser if I'm not a sports coach?
Yes. We support eight organizer roles: coach, parent, teacher, youth pastor, scout leader, gym owner/instructor, organization director, and “anyone else who leads a group.” The challenges adapt — push-ups for sports teams, scripture memorization for youth groups, books read for classrooms, miles run for cross-country, service hours for scouts, etc.
What if I don't see a challenge that fits my group?
You can create custom challenges from the challenge library. Pick the unit (reps, miles, minutes, hours, books, verses, etc.), write a brief description, and you're set. We're also always open to adding more templates — email us with what you'd like to see.
How do players submit their work?
Each player gets a personal link via email. They tap it, log in (no app store install needed — it's a web app), pick a challenge, record a short video, and submit. The submission lands in your review queue. You approve, reject, or adjust the rep count with one tap. Approved reps roll up to the leaderboard.
How do I get sponsors?
We generate a unique QR code for each player. You can download printable flyers (with their name, photo, and code) or share the codes digitally. Sponsors scan, choose flat or per-rep pledging, and pay through Stripe. The money goes to your organization (minus the 3.5% transaction & hosting fee). Most groups have kids share with parents, grandparents, neighbors, coworkers, church friends — the natural circles where they'd normally sell candy bars.
When does the money arrive?
Sponsor pledges are collected in real-time via Stripe as players earn reps. Funds settle to your organization's payout account on Stripe's standard schedule (typically 2 business days after each pledge is processed).
What if a player records something wrong or fakes a rep count?
That's exactly why every submission is video-verified. You review each one before the rep count gets counted. If something looks off — bad camera angle, claimed 50 reps but the video only shows 20 — you can adjust the count or reject with a note. Players see your feedback and can re-record.

For Players

Do I need to download an app?
No. earn²keep runs in any browser on any phone. Your organizer sends you a link, you tap it, and you're in. You can optionally save it to your home screen for one-tap access, but it's never required.
What do I actually do?
Your organizer picks the challenges (push-ups, scripture, books, miles, whatever). You record a short video of yourself doing the activity, tell the app how many reps you did, and submit. Your organizer reviews and approves. Approved reps earn points and money from your sponsors. Easy.
How private is my video?
Your submission videos are private to you and your organizer. They're not posted publicly, not shown on a feed, not shareable as links. Only people with explicit access to your organization's review queue can see them.
I'm under 13. Can I use earn²keep?
With a parent or guardian's permission, yes. We follow COPPA rules for users under 13 — see our Children's Privacy notice for details. Your organizer (or your parent) will be the one managing your account on the back end.

For Sponsors

How do I pledge?
Scan the QR code your player gave you. Pick how much you want to give — either a flat amount (“$25 total”) or a per-rep multiplier (“25¢ per push-up they complete”). Enter your payment info. That's it. We'll send you a receipt and show you the player's progress as they earn.
When am I charged?
For flat pledges, you're charged when you commit. For per-rep pledges, you're charged as the player earns approved reps, up to a cap you set when pledging (so a $0.50/push-up pledge with a $50 cap means you're charged up to $50 max, no matter how many push-ups they do).
Can I get a refund?
Yes — see our refund policy. Pledges are refundable within 30 days for any reason. After 30 days, refunds are case-by-case. Email support@earn2keep.com and we'll work with you.
Is my donation tax-deductible?
Depends on whether the organization you're sponsoring is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit. earn²keep itself isn't a charity — we're the platform. If you need tax documentation, ask the organization you sponsored directly.

Still stuck?

Email support@earn2keep.com and tell us what's going on. We aim to respond within one business day. Include a screenshot if you can — it makes troubleshooting much faster.
