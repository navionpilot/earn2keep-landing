How it works

Two ways to run an event.

Every earn²keep event runs on the same engine — daily challenges, video proof, sponsors, leaderboard, prizes. The difference is the format: one team raising for itself, or many teams competing head-to-head.

Camp

Single-team fundraiser

Your team, your goal, your earnings.

  • One team. One roster, one goal, one fundraising bar.
  • Each player has a minimum. The organizer sets a per-player target. Every player aims for the same number.
  • Bonus points for going over. Pass your minimum and every dollar after that becomes bonus points on the leaderboard.
  • Daily challenges with video proof. Organizer sets the challenges. Players post the videos. Sponsors back what's real.
  • Door prizes for top performers. 1st, 2nd, and 3rd at the end of the event — set by the organizer.
Best for

Sports teams funding camp gear, youth groups planning a mission trip, classrooms saving for a field trip, gyms backing a charity push.

Tournament

Multi-team competition

Multiple teams. One leaderboard. Real stakes.

  • Multiple teams compete. Each team brings its own roster and chases its own goal.
  • Registration fee per team. Teams pay to enter.
  • Same daily-challenge engine. Players post video proof. Sponsors back the work, just like a Camp.
  • Tournament-wide leaderboard. See how every team and every player stacks up against the field.
  • Prizes for top teams + top players. Pool prizes for the winning teams, plus door prizes for star individuals.
Best for

League-wide season campaigns, district tournaments, multi-gym competitions, regional ministry events, church-vs-church challenges.
