What is earn²keep?

Three roles. One simple loop.

A Camp runs four to six weeks. Each day, participants take on a challenge with video proof. Sponsors back the effort. Top performers win prizes. The group keeps what's earned.

01

Organizers

Coaches, teachers, scout leaders, parents, youth pastors, gym owners, org directors — anyone who leads a group. Set up a Camp or Tournament in minutes.

02

Participants

Show up. Do the daily challenge. Post the video. Climb the leaderboard. Hit the goal. Earn the prize.

03

Sponsors

Back someone you believe in. See real verified work. Know your dollars went somewhere real — not into a cookie-dough catalog.
