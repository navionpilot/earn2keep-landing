How Earn2Keep works

1

Compete

Teams and groups register for a 4–6 week Camp or Tournament. Players take on weekly challenges that prove real skill and effort.

2

Get sponsored

Family, friends, and local businesses scan a QR code to fund a player's entry. No cookie dough. No knocking on doors. Just real support for real effort.

3

Earn the win

Top performers win prizes. The team or organization keeps the rest — gear, uniforms, mission trips, and equipment, all earned, not begged for.
