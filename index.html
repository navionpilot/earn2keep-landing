How earn²keep works

A 4–6 week season. Real challenges. Real sponsors. Real earnings.

01

Compete

Teams register for a Camp or Tournament. Players take on weekly challenges that prove real skill, fitness, faith, or service work — verified by their coach.

02

Get Sponsored

Family, friends, and local businesses scan a player's QR code to fund their entry. Sponsors see real verified work — not just a fundraising ask.

03

Keep the Win

Top performers win prizes. The team or organization keeps the rest — gear, mission trips, equipment, uniforms. All earned, not begged for.
