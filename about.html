About

Fundraising that's actually fair to the kids doing the work.

earn²keep was built because the old way of raising money for youth groups, teams, and clubs is broken — for the kids who get sent door-to-door, for the parents who buy candy bars they don't want, and for the organizers who carry the whole thing on their backs.

The problem we kept seeing

Every fall, the same routine: kids hand out cookie dough catalogs, parents pressure-buy from family and coworkers, the team gets a fraction of the take after the supplier's cut, and nobody learns much from the experience. The kids who hustle the hardest aren't rewarded any differently than the ones who let mom and dad handle it. The fundraising company makes most of the money. And the “cause” gets diluted to a sales pitch.

We thought there was a better way: tie the money directly to what the kids actually do. Push-ups. Books read. Scripture memorized. Miles run. Service hours logged. Whatever fits the group. Sponsors pledge a flat amount or a per-rep multiplier. The kids do the work. The money goes where it's supposed to go.

What earn²keep does

earn²keep is the platform that runs that whole flow. Organizers (coaches, parents, teachers, youth pastors, scout leaders, gym owners, directors, anyone leading a group of kids) sign up, create their teams, pick the challenges that make sense for what they're doing, and launch a fundraiser as either a Camp (one team, focused effort) or a Tournament (multiple teams competing for prizes).

Players record short videos of their attempts. Organizers approve, reject, or adjust the rep count with one tap. Approved reps roll up to a live leaderboard. Sponsors who pledged see exactly how their money was earned. Everyone's aligned around effort, not sympathy.

Who we're for

We support eight organizer types out of the gate, because youth groups come in a lot of shapes:

  • Coaches — youth sports teams of any size, any level
  • Teachers — classrooms, school clubs, academic teams
  • Youth pastors — church youth groups, summer programs
  • Scout leaders — troops, packs, dens, crews
  • Gym owners / instructors — youth fitness, martial arts, gymnastics
  • Parents — leading a small group themselves (homeschool co-op, neighborhood team)
  • Organization directors — nonprofits, community programs, youth orgs
  • Anyone else — if you lead a group, you qualify

What makes us different

  • Verified effort. Every approved submission has a video. Sponsors aren't paying for promises — they're paying for proof.
  • Flat fee, not a percentage. $149 for a Camp, $299 for a Tournament. We don't take a cut of the money you raise (just a 3.5% transaction & hosting fee on processed funds — the same kind of payment fee any platform pays).
  • Built for any group. Sports, scripture, books, mileage, service hours, performance arts — if you can count it, you can run it.
  • Kids learn the connection. Reps in. Dollars earned. Real cause and effect. Real money. Real impact.
  • No door knocking. Sponsor QR codes go to the people who care about your kid — and a click is all it takes to pledge.

Where we're headed

We're early. The product works end-to-end, but there's a lot we want to add: AI-assisted verification to take review work off organizers, better sponsor tools, more challenge templates, deeper integrations with the platforms organizations already use. If there's something we're missing that would make this easier for your group, tell us — support@earn2keep.com. We read everything.
